TIC TAC TOE

The classic game of Tic Tac Toe! You are x/X and another player is o/O. Enter your letter and press "Enter", and watch the boxes change to your symbol's color- Pink or Blue. The game ends when a winner gets 3 in a row horizontally, diagonally, or vertically; You can also play again by clicking the button below!
